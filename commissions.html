Bespoke · by commission

Ceramic lighting and custom work for residential and commercial interiors — individually handcrafted by Craig Spencer, developed in collaboration with architects and designers.

01 — Lighting

Pendant
lighting

Spencer Ceramics designs and produces bespoke ceramic pendant lighting for residential and commercial projects. Each piece is individually handcrafted by Craig Spencer, ensuring a high level of refinement and consistency.

All works are developed in collaboration with architects and designers, with careful consideration given to scale, proportion, light quality, materiality, and spatial context. Forms are hand-built in high-fired ceramic, offering controlled geometry, subtle surface variation, and custom glaze finishes aligned to the overall interior palette.

Each piece is conceived as an integrated lighting element — bringing clarity, warmth, and cohesion to the architectural environment.

02 — Lighting

Wall &
feature lighting

Elevate interiors with bespoke ceramic wall and feature lighting, designed and hand-crafted by Craig Spencer.

Each piece is quietly refined — design-led, materially driven, and individually formed. Through a careful balance of proportion, texture, and light, each work is conceived in response to its setting.

The result is a singular object: precise, atmospheric, and enduring — crafted to integrate seamlessly while leaving a lasting impression.

03 — Lighting

Table &
floor lamps

Spencer Ceramics creates bespoke, one-of-a-kind lamps designed for individual spaces. Each piece is hand-crafted with care, balancing sculptural form, materiality, and refined detail.

Designed to perform as beautifully as they appear, every lamp considers both function and atmosphere. Clay bodies, tones, and finishes are tailored to suit each interior context.

In collaboration with specialist electrical technicians, we deliver considered lighting solutions with seamless integration and intuitive control — where every detail is quietly resolved.
